Who We Are

Thank you for your interest in Cavalucci Construction. We take pride in our work and are committed to honesty, loyalty, and integrity in every aspect of our business.

Cavalucci Construction is a home improvement and new home construction company serving Northern Bergen County and the Tri-State area since 1987. Whatever your needs — great or small — the project will be handled with the same professionalism and expert workmanship that is synonymous with our name.

We are continually appraised of and updated on the newest technologies and materials available today. We have organized this website to give you the opportunity to learn about our company.

We are dedicated to completing your project without unnecessary complications or delays. As an interested client, we want you to know that all our work is quality assured and your total satisfaction is guaranteed. You have our word on it!

If we can be of service to you in any phase of residential and commercial work, please contact us to discuss your needs.
